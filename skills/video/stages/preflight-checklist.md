# Preflight checklist

In this first stage, you will be identifying the core needs for this video. Put this information in `video.md` it will be refined in later stages.

Use the structured user input or question tool if available. Otherwise discuss this stage in chat.

- Determine the target length of the video
- Determine the equipment that is available for producing the video
- Determine what platform: YouTube, TikTok, Shorts, internal, etc.
- Determine the Aspect ratio + resolution (16:9, 9:16, 1:1)
- Determine the loudness target for dialogue and other audio
- Determine the people that will be available to work on the video and the hours they have available; talent, crew, etc.
- Determine the budget
- Determine the deadline for posting the video

## Success criteria

- [ ] The budget accomodates all of the people and equipment needed for production.
- [ ] The deadline contains ample time for review and changes
