# Editing and rendering

For this stage of the content creation process you will take all of the production documents and assets created this far and create the final video.

- If the skill is available, use `/remotion-best-practices` to assemble and render the final video
- Try to keep the timing and pace of the video on track
- Start with an initial rough cut of the video and ask for a review.
- Iterate on the edit until everyone is happy with the results.
- Keep an eye out for continuity issues surface them if found.

## Thumbnail creation

- Use the available assets to create the compose thumbnail.
- Make sure its the same aspect ratio and dimensions of the video

## Captions

- Generate captions for this video

## Success criteria

- [ ] The rough cut has been reviewed and includes timestamped notes.
- [ ] The final edit is within +/- 10% of the target time of the video
- [ ] The rendered video is saved to `video.mp4`
- [ ] The thumbnail is saved to `thumbnail.jpg`
- [ ] The captions are in `captions.vtt`
