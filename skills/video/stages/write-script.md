# Writing the script

For this stage you will take the outline in `outline.md` and write a video script and save it to `script.md`.

- Confirm who will be reading the script, suggest an AI voice over if that capability is available.
- Assume a read rate of 150 word per minute unless otherwise specified.
- Make sure the script is timed out to fit within the desired video length.
- The script should be formatted with 50-75 characters per line for easy readability.
- Include each presenters name in all caps before each time they are speaking.
- Include any production cues

## Success criteria

- [ ] All of the assigned talent has a part
- [ ] The script read time is within +/- 10% of the target time
- [ ] Production cues have been added
- [ ] The script has a finalized title

## Examples

Example `script.md`

```md
TITLE: Cheese making and you
READ TIME: [hh:mm:ss]

TOMMY

**(SHOT: medium shot of Tommy)**

Hello I'm Tommy Fry the Cheese Guy and today I'm
showing you how to make a 12 month aged Parmesan
style cheese.

**(ANIMATION: stinger transition to recipe)**

TOMMY

**(SHOT: medium shot of Tommy)**

I'm here in the Cheese Guy Cheese Cave and I've got
my pot and cooktop here. Now I just need to choose
my ingredients.

TOMMY

**(B-ROLL: Pan over ingredients)**

For the Parmesan style cheese we are making today
I'm using raw cow's milk,

**(GRAPHIC: lower third, RAW COW'S MILK)**

salt,

**(GRAPHIC: lower third, SALT)**

and calf rennet.

**(GRAPHIC: lower third, CALF RENNET)**

It's important for you to make sure your ingredients
are fresh.

[...]
```
