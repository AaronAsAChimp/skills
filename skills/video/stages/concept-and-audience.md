# Concept and audience

In this stage you will be defining the concept of the video and how that aligns with the target audience. Update the `video.md` with this information.

Use the structured user input or question tool if available. Otherwise discuss this stage in chat.

## Determining the audience

- Demographics
- Key interests

## Initial concept

- Determine the key performance criteria for the video
- Ask for a description of the video concept
- Iterate on this concept to flesh out any necessary details

Guidelines:

- The video should be engaging
- The video should provide value to the viewer and avoid slop.

## Thumbnail concept

Guidelines

- Prefer immediate impact
- Use bold imagery to promote click throughs.

## Success criteria

- [ ] The concept has a strong basis and creates value for the viewer
- [ ] At least one performance criteria
- [ ] The concept is approved by the key stakeholders

Example `video.md`:

```md
# [Working title of video]

Length: hh:mm:ss

Thumbnail: [describe the thumbnail]

[summary of video concept]

## Additional notes

[additional production notes]
```
