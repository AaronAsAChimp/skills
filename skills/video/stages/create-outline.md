# Creating the outline

In this stage of the content creation process you will be expanding on the video concept laid out in `video.md` and writing an outline that walks through the key beats of the video and save it to `outline.md`;

Start with the key beats of the video.

- Make sure they flow well from one to the next.
- Consider the timing of the video and identify places where beats could be combined or expanded.
- Consider calls for engagement that are suitable for the audience.

Dive deeper into each beat.

- Flesh out each beat with additional details
- Include production notes where graphics, animations, or b-roll should go.
- Include anything that may be important in the script writing stage.

**IMPORTANT** Make sure to check in and see if the video is progressing in the correct direction.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Video has a defined start, middle, and end.
- [ ] There are no loose threads that need to be wrapped up before the end of the video.
- [ ] Each beat has detailed content guidance.
- [ ] The rough estimated time of the video is within +/- 10% of the target time.

Example `outline.md`

```md
# Cheese making and you

- Intro
	- Hello I'm Tommy Fry the Cheese Guy and today I'm showing you how to make cheese
	- SHOT: Medium shot of Tommy reading the intro
	- ANIMATION: stinger transition to recipe
- Recipe
	- Its important to determine what kind of milk, rennet, and enzymes you need for the type of cheese you are making
	- This is the recipe I'm using
	- B-ROLL: Pan over ingredients
	- GRAPHICS: Lower third for ingredient as its being read
- ingredient selection
	- Make sure the ingredients are fresh.
	- Find a good source for these ingredients quality matters.
	- B-ROLL: Farm with cows
- Rennet and enzymes
	- Heat the milk and add the rennet and enzymes you are using
	- The milk will begin to curdle
	- B-ROLL: Milk in pot with ingredients being added
	- B-ROLL: Separating the curd from the whey
- Aging
	- Consider which rind style you will be using and the aging process
	- B-ROLL: Cheese in storage
	- ANIMATION: Stinger with "12 months later" graphic
	- Now look at this beautiful wheel.
	- SHOT: Tommy holding a wheel of cheese
```
